💡 How to use

☰

Open the menu

Tap the three-line button (☰) in the top-left to open the side panel. Switch models, view rate limits, and access links from there.

🤖

Pick a model

All models listed are free on OpenRouter. You can switch mid-conversation — the full history is always forwarded as context.

📡

Streaming responses

Replies stream in real-time, token by token. A blinking cursor shows the model is still generating.

📊

Rate limits

The app tracks 50 requests/day and 20 requests/minute client-side. Counters are saved in your browser and reset at midnight.

🔒

Your API key is safe

The key lives only in the server's .env file. It is never sent to or visible in the browser.
